About us




Welcome to Al Safeena Al Hamra Trading, your premier destination for all your hardware needs in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. With a rich history and a commitment to excellence, we have been serving the community for several years, providing top-quality products and exceptional service. At Al Safeena Al Hamra Trading, we take pride in offering a comprehensive range of hardware products to cater to the diverse needs of our valued customers, Whether you're a professional contractor, a do-it-yourself enthusiast, or simply in search of reliable tools for everyday tasks, we have you covered, coupled with the service of onsite delivery. Our extensive collection includes an array of Power Tools, Hand Tools, Abrasive and Cutting Discs, Switchgears, varieties of Electrical Cables, Bolts and Nuts, General Construction Equipment, Generators, Air Compressors, Safety Equipment, House Tools, Paints, Furniture Fittings, Wrenches, Switches, Extensions, Pneumatic Tools and much more. From shovels and hand levels to safety gear and welding tools, we stock everything you need to complete your projects efficiently and safely.

Additionally, we are pleased to announce that Al Safeena Al Hamra Trading now also provides hardware maintenance services for all types of hardware. Our team of skilled technicians is available to address any issues you may encounter, ensuring that your tools and equipment are functioning optimally. We offer onsite support, and our technicians can be dispatched if required, providing you with peace of mind and convenience.

Founded by Mr. Shabbir Husain Zariwala, Al Safeena Al Hamra Trading has become synonymous with trust and reliability in the hardware industry. With his visionary leadership and expertise, we have grown steadily, earning a reputation for delivering exceptional products and services. We understand that quality matters to our customers, which is why we source our products from trusted manufacturers who share our commitment to excellence. We believe in offering only the finest tools and equipment that meet the highest standards of durability, performance, and safety. At Al Safeena Al Hamra Trading, we are not just a hardware store; we are your partners in success. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is always ready to assist you, providing expert advice and guidance to help you make informed decisions. We strive to create a personalized shopping experience, ensuring that you find the right tools and equipment to meet your specific requirements. We are conveniently located in Sharjah, making it easy for you to visit our store and explore our extensive inventory. Alternatively, you can also browse our website, where you'll find detailed product information and the convenience of online shopping.

Thank you for choosing Al Safeena Al Hamra Trading as your trusted hardware provider. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your success as you tackle your projects with confidence and efficiency.
